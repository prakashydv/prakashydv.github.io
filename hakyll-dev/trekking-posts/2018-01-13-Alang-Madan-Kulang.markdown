#The Grand AMK Trail

Once again another weekend invested with SG Trekkers, this time the famous AMK (Alang-Madan-Kulang) Trail. We boarded the private traveller bus at 21:00 from Shivaji Nagar. After some detours within the city to procure sleeping bags and pick up remaining members, we ejected from Pune around 23:00. I was soon asleep in the comfy bus and woke early at around 5:30 in the morning when the bus was at base village. After answering natures call in a frangrant field under a thin silver moon, we devoured our share of poha and were off by 7:00. Trudging through the flat valley terrain we started ascending towards Alang, the A of the AMK trail.

Alang trail is mostly traverse. There is even a ladder at one patch, a rusted shaking menace.
